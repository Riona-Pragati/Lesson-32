A little bit about me

Crocheting is a very good hobby for teens. It is a lot of fun to do and it helps us make useful things like atote bag, accessories and decorations.


In today’s age, crochetinghas become an essential skill. Crocheting for kids helps them interact with technology better. Studies also show that it aids in holistic development of the child, improving cognitive functioning as well as logical thinking. Holistic development means evolving cognitive, physical, and social skills of a child to prepare them better for the interactions with the world. Coding as an activity not only involves mental development but also focuses on social understanding.
